This privacy policy describes how Kodaskills, SASU, collects and processes the personal data of users of the Kodaskills website.

## 1. Data Controller

The data controller is: **Kodaskills, SASU**

Registered office: 60 rue François Ier, 75008 Paris, France

Contact: [legal@kodaskills.co](mailto:legal@kodaskills.co)

## 2. Data Collected

Depending on how you use the Site, Kodaskills may collect the following categories of data:

- identification and contact details submitted via the contact form;
- content of messages sent;
- technical browsing data;
- browsing preferences stored locally;
- audience and usage data, depending on the configuration of measurement tools.

## 3. Purposes of Processing

Data is processed for the following purposes:

- responding to requests submitted via the contact form;
- managing pre-contractual or professional exchanges;
- ensuring the technical operation of the Site;
- remembering browsing preferences;
- measuring audience, analysing Site usage, and improving its content;
- ensuring Site security and preventing abuse.

## 4. Legal Bases

Depending on the case, processing is based on:

- the legitimate interest of Kodaskills in responding to requests and administering its Site;
- the performance of pre-contractual measures when a user requests a service;
- the user's consent when required, in particular for certain trackers or analytics tools;
- compliance with legal obligations where applicable.

## 5. Recipients

Data is shared, as needed, with Kodaskills and its authorised technical service providers.

The Site is hosted by GitHub.

Analytics or audience measurement providers may also be involved, in particular PostHog, depending on the Site's configuration.

## 6. Retention Periods

Kodaskills retains data for a period proportionate to the purposes pursued. As a guide:

- contact requests are retained for the duration necessary to process the request, then for a period compatible with professional relationship follow-up;
- data required to evidence a contractual or pre-contractual relationship may be retained longer if justified;
- browsing preferences stored via localStorage are retained until deleted by the user or technical expiry;
- audience data is retained for a limited period in accordance with the settings of the tool used and applicable requirements.

## 7. Cookies, localStorage, and Trackers

The Site may use localStorage to retain certain browsing preferences.

Where non-strictly necessary trackers are used, their placement or reading is subject to the user's prior consent if required by applicable regulations.

The user may configure their browser to block all or part of these technologies, but this may affect certain Site functionalities.

## 8. PostHog

Kodaskills may use PostHog to measure audience and improve the user experience.

Depending on the configuration chosen, this tool may process browsing data and use trackers.

Where required, the user's consent is obtained before activating the tool.

## 9. Security

Kodaskills implements reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against loss, unauthorised access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Data transmitted on the Site is, when the site is configured with HTTPS, protected by encryption in transit. Despite these measures, no system offers absolute security.

## 10. Transfers Outside the European Union

Depending on the tools used, some data may be hosted or accessed from countries outside the European Union.

In such cases, Kodaskills ensures that appropriate safeguards are in place when required by applicable regulations.

## 11. Individual Rights

In accordance with applicable regulations, users have the following rights, depending on the case:

- right of access;
- right of rectification;
- right to erasure;
- right to restriction of processing;
- right to object;
- right to data portability;
- right to withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

## 12. Exercising Rights

To exercise their rights, users may write to: [legal@kodaskills.co](mailto:legal@kodaskills.co).

Kodaskills may request proof of identity in case of reasonable doubt about the requester's identity.

Users may also lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority (CNIL in France, or the authority of their country of residence).

## 13. Updates

This policy may be amended at any time to reflect legal, technical, or functional changes to the Site.
